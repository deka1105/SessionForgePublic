## Production notes

- Pre-stage two accounts on the demo site so you don't burn seconds typing real passwords — use a password manager, autofill, or fake it with a recorded clip.
- Keep the cursor moves snappy. Cut, don't pan.
- The sidebar color-dot is your hero motif — make sure the two identities use clearly different colors (e.g. pink and blue) so the viewer's eye instantly groups tabs to identities.
- End frame should hold for at least 1.5s so people can read the repo URL.
